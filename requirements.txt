## Explaination
1. opencv-python: For frame manipulation (e.g., extracting subframes and enhancing them).
2. torch: Required for running GFPGAN and CodeFormer models.
3. numpy: For numerical operations (if needed for frame calculations).
4. moviepy: To handle video and audio merging or editing.
5. ffmpeg-python: Wrapper for FFmpeg to process video files.
